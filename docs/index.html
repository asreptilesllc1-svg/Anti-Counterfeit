How it works

Three steps. No hardware, no proprietary scanners.

01 — TAG

Generate a signed QR for every item

Each product gets a unique, cryptographically signed code — impossible to forge without the platform's private signing key, which never leaves our secured infrastructure.

02 — SCAN

Customers verify with their own phone

No app to install. A camera scan opens an instant, branded verification page.

03 — VERIFY

Get an answer, and a record

Authentic or not, every scan is logged — timestamp, location signal, and risk level — automatically.
